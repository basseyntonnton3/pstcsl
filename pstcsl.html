Who We Are

About PSTCSL

The Private School Teachers Cooperative Society (PSTCSL) represents a collective of dedicated, qualified, and professional private school educators across Nigeria. We are united in our commitment to advancing the quality of private education while ensuring that teachers are treated with dignity, fairness, and respect.

01

Our Identity

A registered and legally recognized professional body comprising private school teachers across all levels of education, from early childhood to secondary schools, operating throughout Nigeria.

  • Qualified and certified teachers
  • Experienced educators across disciplines
  • Educational administrators and coordinators
  • Early career teachers committed to excellence
02

The Challenge

Private school teachers face numerous systemic challenges:

  • Inadequate and irregular compensation
  • Poor working conditions
  • Limited access to healthcare
  • Job insecurity and unfair practices
  • Absence of professional development
03

Our Advocacy

We champion comprehensive welfare that includes:

  • Financial security and fair compensation
  • Health insurance and medical support
  • Pension and retirement benefits
  • Professional development opportunities
  • Job security and labor rights protection
